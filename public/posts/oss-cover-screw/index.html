產品介紹 Product Introduction

  1. 密封與保護植體內部 覆蓋螺絲的主要功能是完全封閉植體內部的連接孔，防止手術後癒合期間食物殘渣、唾液及細菌進入。這能有效降低植體內部感染的風險，確保植體與骨頭的整合（Osseointegration）不受干擾。

  2. 促進軟組織覆蓋癒合 在「兩階段手術」中，覆蓋螺絲體積極小，其超薄的頂部設計允許牙齦組織完全覆蓋並縫合。這能提供一個封閉且穩定的環境，讓植體在無負擔的情況下生長。

  3. 精密配對，防止微動 針對 Osstem TSIII/TSIV 系統設計，具備高精度的螺紋與緊密貼合的邊緣，能確保與植體平台完美對接，防止因受力產生的微小震動導致螺絲鬆脫。

  4. 醫學級高強度鈦合金 採用生物相容性極佳的鈦合金（Grade 5 Titanium）製成，具有優異的抗腐蝕性與機械強度。表面平滑處理可減少組織黏附，便於二階段手術時輕鬆移除。

  5. 矮平化設計 (Low-Profile Design) 極薄的頂部厚度設計，能減少對上方軟組織的張力與壓迫，降低傷口裂開或螺絲穿出牙齦的機率。

  1. Sealing and Internal Protection The primary function of the cover screw is to completely seal the internal connection of the implant. This prevents food debris, saliva, and bacteria from entering the implant site during the healing phase, minimizing the risk of internal infection and ensuring successful osseointegration.

  2. Supporting Submerged Healing In a two-stage surgical protocol, the low-profile design allows the gingival tissue to be completely sutured over the implant. This creates a closed and stable environment, shielding the implant from functional loading during the initial healing period.

  3. Precision Fit and Micro-Movement Prevention Engineered specifically for the Osstem TSIII/TSIV systems, featuring high-precision threading and a tight marginal fit. This ensures perfect alignment with the implant platform, preventing loosening due to micro-movements.

  4. Medical-Grade High-Strength Titanium Manufactured from biocompatible Grade 5 Titanium alloy, offering superior corrosion resistance and mechanical durability. The smooth surface finish reduces tissue adherence, allowing for easy removal during the second-stage surgery.

  5. Low-Profile Design The ultra-thin head thickness reduces tension and pressure on the overlying soft tissue, lowering the risk of wound dehiscence or premature screw exposure through the gingiva.
