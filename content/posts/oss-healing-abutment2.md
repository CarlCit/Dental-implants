產品功能介紹：Osstem 系統癒合基台Product Introduction: Osstem System Healing Abutment繁體中文（台灣繁體）1. 精準形塑萌出輪廓 (Emergence Profile)癒合基台的主要任務是在植體植入後的癒合期間，引導牙齦組織生長。透過多樣化的直徑與高度選擇，能預先為未來的正式牙冠創造出自然、美觀且易於清潔的牙齦溝槽空間。2. 完善的系統相容性專為 Osstem TSIII / TSIV 系統設計，具備高精度的內八角（Internal Hex）連接結構。區分為 Mini (M) 與 Regular (R) 兩種平台規格，確保與植體平台緊密貼合，防止細菌滲入植體內部。3. 簡化臨床操作與識別基台頂端刻有清晰的規格標記（如直徑與穿齦高度），方便醫師在臨床操作時快速辨識。其精密加工的螺紋設計可確保鎖固後的穩定性，避免癒合期間發生鬆脫。4. 醫療級鈦合金與表面處理採用高生物相容性的 Grade 5 鈦合金（Ti-6Al-4V ELI）製成。表面經過精細拋光，能有效減少牙菌斑與生物膜的附著，降低植體周圍炎（Peri-implantitis）的風險。5. 豐富的規格組合提供多種直徑（$\varnothing 4.0$ 到 $\varnothing 7.0$）與穿齦高度（2mm 到 9mm），能滿足從前牙美觀區到後牙大臼齒區等各種不同的臨床解剖需求。English Version1. Precise Shaping of the Emergence ProfileThe primary role of the healing abutment is to guide the soft tissue during the healing phase. By offering various diameters and heights, it creates a natural and esthetic gingival architecture, ensuring optimal space for the final prosthetic restoration.2. Complete System CompatibilityEngineered specifically for Osstem TSIII / TSIV systems, featuring high-precision internal hex connections. Available in Mini (M) and Regular (R) platforms, it ensures a tight marginal seal to prevent bacterial colonization within the implant.3. Simplified Clinical IdentificationThe top surface is laser-etched with clear specification markings (Diameter and Gingival Height), allowing for quick identification during clinical procedures. The precision-engineered threads ensure stable fixation and prevent loosening during the healing period.4. Medical-Grade Titanium & Surface FinishManufactured from high-biocompatibility Grade 5 Titanium Alloy (Ti-6Al-4V ELI). The high-polish surface finish minimizes plaque accumulation and biofilm formation, reducing the risk of peri-implantitis.5. Extensive Range of SpecificationsAvailable in a wide range of diameters ($\varnothing 4.0$ to $\varnothing 7.0$) and gingival heights (2mm to 9mm) to accommodate diverse clinical scenarios, from the esthetic zone to the molar region.
