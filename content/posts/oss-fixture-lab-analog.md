Product Introduction: Osstem System Fixture Lab Analog
繁體中文（台灣繁體）
1. 精確模擬植體空間位置
植體替代體的主要功能是在牙科技工所製作的石膏模型中，精確地「代表」患者口中的植體。它能還原植體在牙床中的三維位置、深度以及內八角（Internal Hex）的角度，確保技工端製作的基台與牙冠能精準鎖固。

2. 卓越的固位與穩定性
外部設計具有深層的固位溝槽與切平位，當替代體被石膏包裹時，能提供極強的抓地力，有效防止在旋緊螺絲或進行磨削加工時發生旋轉或位移。

3. 顏色編碼識別 (Color-Coding)
為了減少臨床與技工端的溝通誤差，替代體表面採用奧齒泰標準顏色識別：

黃色 (Yellow)： 對應 Mini (M) 平台（適用於直徑 3.5 植體）。

綠色 (Green)： 對應 Regular (R) 平台（適用於直徑 4.0 及以上植體）。

4. 高強度不鏽鋼材質
採用高等級醫療不鏽鋼製造，具備極高的硬度與耐磨性。即使經過多次取模組件的插拔與螺絲鎖固，接口處仍能保持原始精度，不會輕易變形。

5. 完美相容 TS 系統
專為 Osstem TSIII / TSIV 系統設計，確保與原廠及高品質相容的轉移桿（Transfer Abutment）達到無間隙的適配效果。

English Version
1. Precise Replication of Implant Position
The Fixture Lab Analog is designed to "represent" the actual implant within the laboratory stone model. It accurately replicates the 3D position, depth, and internal hex orientation, ensuring that the final abutment and crown fabricated by the technician will fit perfectly in the patient's mouth.

2. Superior Retention and Stability
The exterior features deep retention grooves and a flat-side design. Once embedded in the dental stone, these features provide exceptional stability, preventing any rotation or vertical movement during laboratory procedures such as screw tightening or adjustments.

3. Intuitive Color-Coding System
To streamline the workflow and minimize errors, the analogs follow Osstem’s standard color-coding:

Yellow: Indicates the Mini (M) platform (for Ø 3.5 implants).

Green: Indicates the Regular (R) platform (for Ø 4.0 and above implants).

4. High-Strength Stainless Steel Construction
Manufactured from premium medical-grade stainless steel, offering high hardness and excellent wear resistance. This ensures that the internal connection maintains its precision even after repeated insertion of transfer components and screw tightening.

5. Seamless TS System Compatibility
Specifically engineered for Osstem TSIII / TSIV systems, ensuring a zero-play fit with both original and high-quality compatible transfer abutments.
