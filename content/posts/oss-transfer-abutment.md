## 產品介紹 <small>Product Introduction</small>

我們是專業做第三方種植配件的廠家

適配 Osstem TS 系統

 繁體中文 (台灣繁體)
1. **高效的黏著修復方案**：成品基台專為黏著式假牙設計，具備理想的錐度與表面粗糙度，能與牙冠形成強大的機械固位力，適用於多數單顆或多顆缺牙的修復案例。
2. **精密內八角連接結構**：專為 Osstem **TSIII / TSIV** 系統研發，提供 **Hex (六角)** 與 **Non-Hex (非六角)** 兩種版本。六角設計具備卓越的防轉動性能，而非六角設計則方便多顆連冠的置入。
3. **優化的萌出輪廓**：提供多種穿齦高度 (Gingival Height) 選擇，能適應病患不同的牙齦厚度，確保假牙邊緣與牙齦完美銜接，達到美觀與生物相容性的平衡。
4. **醫療級高強度鈦合金**：採用 Grade 5 鈦合金材質，具備極高的機械強度與抗疲勞特性，能承受長期的咬合壓力，確保修復體的長期穩定性。

 English Version
1. **Efficient Cement-Retained Solution**: Specifically designed for cement-retained prostheses, featuring an optimized taper and surface texture to ensure superior mechanical retention for crowns in both single and multi-unit cases.
2. **Precision Internal Hex Connection**: Engineered for Osstem **TSIII / TSIV** systems, available in **Hex** and **Non-Hex** versions. The Hex design offers excellent anti-rotational stability, while the Non-Hex version facilitates the path of insertion for bridges.
3. **Optimized Emergence Profile**: A wide range of gingival heights is available to accommodate various gingival thicknesses, ensuring a seamless and esthetic transition between the prosthesis and the soft tissue.
4. **Medical-Grade High-Strength Titanium**: Manufactured from Grade 5 Titanium alloy, providing exceptional mechanical strength and fatigue resistance to withstand long-term occlusal forces.
