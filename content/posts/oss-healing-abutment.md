## 產品介紹 <small>Product Introduction</small>

1. 完美適配 Osstem TS/GS 系統 專為 Osstem 植體系統設計，具備高精度的內八角（Internal Hex）連接結構，確保與 TSIII、TSIV 等主流型號完美對位，減少零件間的微動，提升癒合品質。

2. 理想的牙齦溝槽成型 透過多樣化的穿齦高度與直徑組合，能精準形塑出符合 Osstem 假牙組件的「萌出輪廓」（Emergence Profile），為後續的取模與正式基台安裝奠定良好基礎。

3. 簡化二階段操作流程 頂端設有清晰的規格標記（如直徑與高度），方便臨床醫師快速辨識。其獨特的螺紋設計能確保鎖固力量均勻，避免在癒合期間發生鬆脫。

4. 卓越的生物相容性 採用醫療級鈦合金材質，表面經過精密拋光處理，能減少牙菌斑附著，並促進軟組織圍繞基台健康生長，降低植體周圍發炎的風險。

5. 完整的臨床規格 提供 Mini、Regular 與 Wide 各種平台的選擇，覆蓋從前牙美觀區到後牙磨牙區的所有臨床需求。
-
1. Seamless Compatibility with Osstem TS/GS Systems Specifically engineered for the Osstem implant system, featuring high-precision internal hex connections. This ensures a perfect fit with mainstream models like TSIII and TSIV, minimizing micro-movements and optimizing healing outcomes.

2. Optimal Gingival Sulcus Contouring With a wide array of gingival heights and diameters, it precisely shapes the emergence profile to match Osstem prosthetic components, streamlining the process for future impression-taking and final abutment placement.

3. Simplified Clinical Workflow The top surface features clear specification markings (e.g., diameter and height) for quick clinical identification. The precision-engineered thread design ensures stable fixation and prevents loosening during the healing phase.

4. Superior Biocompatibility Manufactured from medical-grade titanium alloy with a high-polish surface finish. This design minimizes plaque accumulation and encourages healthy soft tissue integration around the abutment, reducing the risk of peri-implantitis.

5. Comprehensive Clinical Specifications Available in Mini, Regular, and Wide platform options to meet all clinical requirements, ranging from the esthetic zone (anterior) to the molar region (posterior).
