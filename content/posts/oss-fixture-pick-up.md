## 產品介紹 <small>Product Introduction</small>

我們是專業做第三方種植配件的廠家

1. 高精度位置轉移
轉移桿的主要功能是在取模過程中，將病患口腔內植體的精確三維位置（包含深度、角度與六角對位）轉移到石膏模型上，確保後續假牙製作的精密適配。

2. 開口式取模 (Open-Tray / Pick-up Type)

功能： 取模後轉移桿會直接「埋入」印模材中，隨牙托一起取出。

優點： 適用於植體角度較偏斜或多顆植體同時取模的情況，能提供極高的穩定度與精確度，減少變形風險。

特色： 配有加長型固位螺絲，方便從開口牙托上方鬆脫。

3. 閉口式取模 (Closed-Tray / Transfer Type)

功能： 取模後轉移桿留在口腔中，待牙托取出後，再手動將轉移桿旋下並「插回」印模材中。

優點： 適用於後牙區張口度受限的患者，操作簡便，不需額外加工牙托。

特色： 具備精確的平面定位設計，確保回插時的對位準確。

4. 完美適配 Osstem TS 系統
針對 Osstem TSIII/TSIV 研發，區分為 Mini 與 Regular 平台，接口處經過精密切削，確保與植體內八角結構緊密結合，杜絕任何旋轉間隙。

English Version
1. High-Precision Position Transfer
The primary role of the transfer abutment is to accurately replicate the 3D position (depth, angulation, and hex orientation) of the implant from the patient's mouth to the laboratory master model, ensuring a passive fit for the final prosthesis.

2. Open-Tray Technique (Pick-up Type)

Function: The abutment remains embedded in the impression material and is "picked up" when the tray is removed.

Advantages: Ideal for malaligned implants or multi-unit cases. It offers superior stability and precision by minimizing potential distortion.

Features: Includes an extended guide screw for easy access and loosening through the open tray.

3. Closed-Tray Technique (Transfer Type)

Function: The abutment stays in the implant after the impression is removed; it is then unscrewed and "transferred" back into the impression tray.

Advantages: Best suited for posterior regions with limited inter-occlusal space. It is user-friendly and requires no special tray modifications.

Features: Designed with precise flat-side orientation to ensure accurate re-insertion.

4. Seamless Osstem TS Compatibility
Specifically designed for Osstem TSIII/TSIV systems (Mini & Regular platforms). The high-tolerance connection ensures a snug fit with the internal hex, eliminating any rotational play.
