產品功能介紹：Osstem 系統複合替代體 (Esthetic-low Lab Analog)
Product Introduction: Osstem System Esthetic-low Lab Analog
繁體中文（台灣繁體）
1. 高精度口腔位置還原
複合替代體的主要功能是在牙技工所的模型中，「代表」患者口中的植體。它能精確複製植體在牙床中的位置、深度以及六角（Hex）對位，是製作高品質全瓷冠或混合基台（Hybrid Abutment）的基礎。

2. 強化定位與抗轉動設計
Esthetic-low 系列具備特殊的切平位與固位溝槽，當埋入石膏模型中時，能提供卓越的穩定性，防止在製作假牙過程（如鎖固螺絲、拋光冠體）中發生任何微小的旋轉或鬆動。

3. 顏色識別系統
為了簡化技工室的工作流程，產品外觀通常採用顏色編碼（Color-coded）。例如：黃色對應 Mini (M) 平台，綠色對應 Regular (R) 平台，讓技工師能一眼辨識規格，避免誤用。

4. 醫療級耐用材質
採用高等級不鏽鋼或鈦合金製成，具備極佳的耐磨損性。即使在模型製作過程中反覆取放轉移桿（Transfer Abutment），也能保持接口處的原始精度，不因磨損導致適配誤差。

5. 完美適配 TSIII / TSIV 系統
針對 Osstem 主流植體系統量身打造，確保與原廠或高品質相容配件的內八角（Internal Hex）結構達到「零間隙」配合。

English Version
1. High-Precision Oral Position Replication
The primary function of the lab analog is to "represent" the actual implant within the laboratory stone model. It accurately replicates the implant's position, depth, and hex orientation, serving as the essential foundation for fabricating high-quality crowns and hybrid abutments.

2. Enhanced Stability and Anti-Rotational Design
The Esthetic-low series features unique flat-side and retention grooves. Once embedded in the dental stone, these provide superior stability, preventing any micro-rotation or movement during laboratory procedures such as screw tightening or crown polishing.

3. Intuitive Color-Coding System
To streamline the laboratory workflow, these analogs are color-coded for easy identification. For instance, yellow signifies the Mini (M) platform, while green indicates the Regular (R) platform, minimizing the risk of selecting the wrong component.

4. Medical-Grade Durable Material
Manufactured from high-grade stainless steel or titanium alloy, offering exceptional wear resistance. This ensures that the internal connection maintains its original precision even after repeated insertion and removal of transfer components.

5. Seamless Integration with TSIII / TSIV Systems
Specially engineered for Osstem's leading implant systems, ensuring a "zero-play" fit with the internal hex structure of both original and high-quality compatible prosthetic components.
