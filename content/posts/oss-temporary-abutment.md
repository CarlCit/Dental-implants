Product Introduction: Osstem System Temporary Abutment
繁體中文（台灣繁體）
1. 立即負重與美觀恢復
臨時基台專為「即刻負重」（Immediate Loading）或二階段手術後的臨時修復設計。醫師可現場製作臨時牙冠並黏著於基台，讓病患在植牙當天或短時間內即可恢復門面美觀。

2. 理想的牙齦塑形（Emergence Profile）
透過臨時假牙的邊緣設計，臨時基台能持續引導軟組織癒合，形塑出自然且美觀的牙齦溝槽，為後續永久性基台與牙冠的安裝預留完美空間。

3. 高生物相容性材質
採用醫療級鈦合金 (Titanium) 製成，具備優異的組織親和力。基台表面經過精密處理，能有效減少細菌附著，保護植體周圍組織健康。

4. 易於修整與調整
基台柱體設計便於臨床醫師根據患者的咬合空間，進行切削與高度調整，並提供極佳的樹脂黏結力，確保臨時假牙穩固不脫落。

5. 區分六角與非六角設計

六角 (Hex)： 具備防轉動機制，適用於「單顆」缺牙修復。

非六角 (Non-Hex)： 適用於「多顆牙橋」修復，方便調整多個植體間的平行度與置入徑。

English Version
1. Immediate Loading and Esthetic Restoration
The temporary abutment is specifically designed for "Immediate Loading" protocols or temporary restoration after second-stage surgery. It allows clinicians to fabricate and cement a temporary crown chairside, restoring the patient's smile and confidence quickly.

2. Ideal Gingival Contouring (Emergence Profile)
By supporting a provisional crown, the temporary abutment helps shape and maintain the soft tissue architecture during the healing phase, ensuring a natural-looking emergence profile for the final restoration.

3. High Biocompatibility Material
Manufactured from medical-grade Titanium alloy, providing excellent tissue integration. The precision-machined surface minimizes plaque accumulation and protects the peri-implant environment.

4. Easy Modification and Adjustment
The post design allows clinicians to easily trim and adjust the height according to the available occlusal space. It provides superior bonding strength with acrylic or composite resins for a stable provisional result.

5. Hex vs. Non-Hex Options

Hex: Features an anti-rotational mechanism, ideal for "single-unit" restorations.

Non-Hex: Designed for "multi-unit" bridges, allowing for easier path of insertion and alignment between multiple implants.
